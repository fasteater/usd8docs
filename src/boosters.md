# Boosters
<br/><img src="/assets/booster.png" width="600px" /><br/>

Boosters are transferable NFTs that can be burned when filing a claim to add a 1% boost to your total Cover Score. Boosters can only be used after USD8 is launched and the Cover Pool is in operation; however, you can start collecting Boosters now.<br/><br/>
Boosters are stackable — e.g. 2 Boosters give you 2%. No limits.


# Distribution
Boosters are not for sale. They are distributed only to users who help USD8 grow. Our team monitors social channels like X.com and YouTube, and will reach out with an offer if you post about USD8 (as long as it isn't spam).

You can also send links to your posts in our [Telegram group](https://t.me/+e84i2oYk1ao1MTk1) or ping [USD8 on X.com](https://x.com/usd8_fi) to request Boosters.

Happy collecting.
